Mars

Mars is a red dwarf planet located in the outer reaches of the solar system. It is the fourth planet from the sun and the second-smallest planet in the solar system.

Earth

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only known planet to harbor life. It is also the only known planet with liquid water on its surface.
